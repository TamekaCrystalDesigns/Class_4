Hello, my name is Tameka. I'm currently the Online Media Specialist at NYU-ePoly, among top-ranked grad engineering schools by US News & World Report. I handle all creative requests for digital and print materials for upcoming events, website design and development, as well as website maintenance.

In addition, to my web and creative support I have managed several Google AdWords and Geo-Targeting campaigns. I also serve as a representative to NYU-ePoly's corporate partners and travel to partnership and client events to assist with marketing outreach and graduate student recruitment efforts.

My background and experience consists of: Online Interactive Ad Marketing, Advertising, Broadcast Journalism, and Online Media in Higher Education. Prior to my current position, I started my own freelance web and graphic design business which I managed for approximately two years. I have a Bachelor of Arts in Professional Journalism, Broadcast and a Web Design Certification. I have also completed certified Google AdWords training.
